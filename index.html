Frequently asked
questions

What is included in the program?
  • Daily classes conducted by certified experts and mentors.
  • Interactive sessions with industry experts and Vtin's experienced members.
  • Individual projects to assess growth and understanding.
  • Dedicated problem-solving classes for clearing doubts and receiving feedback.
How much does Vtin Academy charge for its programs?
  • Registration for all Vtin Academy courses is free!
  • Upon successfully clearing the Vtin Academy Entrance Exam (VAEE), there is a nominal enrolment charge of INR 14999/- to cover operational costs.
What is the duration of the Vtin Academy program?
The program involves 2 to 3 hours of training per day, 5 days a week, for a total duration of 8 to 12 weeks.
What are the eligibility criteria for Vtin Academy?
  • Fresh graduates from relevant branches can apply.
  • Last semester students without any live ATKT (Backlog) are eligible to apply.
  • The aggregate CGPA should be greater than 6.0.
  • Experienced candidates with up to 2 years of experience can also apply.
What is the selection process?
  • Candidates go through an online test consisting of aptitude and basic skill assessment, as well as logic and complex skill assessment.
  • Candidates clearing the Vtin Academy Entrance Examination (VAEE) and HR screening become eligible to join Vtin Academy.
What happens if I miss a lecture?
We highly recommend attending live sessions to interact with trainers and clarify doubts. However, in case of exams or emergencies, session recordings are shared with students.
Will I receive a certificate upon completing the program?
Yes, a certificate of completion will be provided upon successful submission of your project.
Which companies will I be referred to after completing the Vtin Academy Program?
Our expert placement team will guide you in finding suitable opportunities with MNCs and fast-growing startups in India.
Is job placement guaranteed after completing the program?
Vtin Academy is committed to providing placement assistance. We conduct resume-building sessions and mock interviews to enhance your chances of securing your dream job. However, job placement cannot be guaranteed.
Who can I contact for further questions?
For any queries, please feel free to reach out to us at support@Vtin.academy.
